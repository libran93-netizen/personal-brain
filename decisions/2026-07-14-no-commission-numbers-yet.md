# Decision: Don't quote commission terms yet

**Context:** TON's revenue model isn't locked, but Karan is already talking to leaders and operators across five regions. The commission question will come up in every onboarding conversation.
**Decision:** Never quote a commission number while the model is undecided. Instead, frame early leaders as partners helping shape fair terms.
**Consequences:** Early conversations stay honest — no number gets promised that the eventual model might contradict — and joining early is positioned as influence rather than a discount. The field-leader-onboarding skill encodes this framing so it's applied consistently in outreach and calls.
