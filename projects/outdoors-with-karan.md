# Outdoors with Karan

<!--STATUS:BEGIN-->
- First recorded status: outdoorswithkaran.com is live, and its /experience section (built in Antigravity) currently hosts The Outdoor Network's Phase-0 platform — guide discovery, AI trip planning, and 13 Himalayan routes.
- Monetization on the site is traveler-pays contact unlocks (₹499 single / ₹1,499 annual) with a 48-hour response guarantee; whether that guarantee actually triggers refunds or is only UI copy is flagged as something to verify before more real guides' numbers go behind it.
- The pending v2 change plan lands mostly on this site: an honest guide/operator identity model (personally-guides vs runs-a-team), consent-based verification, and real Privacy Policy, Terms, and Guide Agreement content before wider onboarding.
- The standalone "Outdoor Professional Platform" job board idea is folded into this site as a "Become a Listed Guide" tab rather than a separate product.
