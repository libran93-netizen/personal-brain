# Decision: Blue Sheep stays off the TON platform through Phase 0–1

**Context:** Karan owns both the platform and Blue Sheep Adventures, so guides could reasonably suspect the marketplace favors the house brand. The v2 change plan (which Karan shared this window) posed two options: list Blue Sheep with publicly-stated neutral ranking, or keep it off entirely.
**Decision:** Option A — Blue Sheep Adventures is not listed on the platform at all through Phase 0–1. Recorded as approved in the v2 change plan.
**Consequences:** Removes the conflict-of-interest objection during early guide recruitment, which matters while trust is the platform's main asset. Blue Sheep continues operating independently off-platform; listing it can be revisited later once public ranking rules exist.
